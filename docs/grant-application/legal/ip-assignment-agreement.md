## Background

A. The Company is developing a multiplayer video game known as "Tech World" (the "Project").

B. The Contributor has provided, or will provide, services to the Company in connection with the Project in the role of [Consultant / Level Designer / other role] (the "Services").

C. The parties wish to confirm that all intellectual property created by the Contributor in connection with the Project is owned by the Company.

## Agreed Terms

### 1. Definitions

**"Intellectual Property"** means all intellectual property rights, including copyright, patents, trademarks, designs, trade secrets, know-how, and all other rights of a similar nature, whether registered or unregistered, and including applications for registration of any such rights, anywhere in the world.

**"Project Materials"** means all works, materials, code, designs, artwork, documentation, ideas, concepts, and other outputs created, developed, or contributed by the Contributor in connection with the Project, whether created before or after the date of this Agreement.

### 2. Assignment of Intellectual Property

2.1 The Contributor hereby assigns to the Company, with full title guarantee, all right, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property in the Project Materials, including all existing and future rights, for the full duration of those rights and any extensions or renewals, throughout the world.

2.2 To the extent that any Intellectual Property in the Project Materials does not vest in the Company automatically by operation of clause 2.1, the Contributor holds such Intellectual Property on trust for the Company and will assign it to the Company on request.

2.3 The Contributor agrees to execute any further documents and do any further acts that may be reasonably required by the Company to give effect to this clause.

### 3. Moral Rights

3.1 To the extent permitted by law, the Contributor consents to the Company and its licensees and successors in title using, modifying, and dealing with the Project Materials without identifying the Contributor as author and without requiring the Contributor's consent, including any acts that might otherwise infringe the Contributor's moral rights under Part IX of the Copyright Act 1968 (Cth).

3.2 This consent is genuine, given voluntarily, and extends to acts occurring before and after the date of this Agreement.

### 4. Warranties

The Contributor warrants that:

(a) the Project Materials are original works created by the Contributor;

(b) the Project Materials do not infringe the Intellectual Property rights of any third party;

(c) the Contributor has full right and authority to enter into this Agreement and assign the Intellectual Property as described;

(d) the Contributor has not previously assigned, licensed, or encumbered the Intellectual Property in the Project Materials in any way that would conflict with this Agreement.

### 5. Consideration

The Contributor acknowledges that the consideration for this Agreement includes:

(a) [the fees paid or to be paid to the Contributor for the Services under separate arrangement]; and/or

(b) the mutual benefits of the Contributor's participation in the Project.

### 6. No Employment Relationship

Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment relationship between the Company and the Contributor. The Contributor provides Services as an independent contractor.

### 7. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Victoria, Australia. The parties submit to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Victoria.

### 8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in relation to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and representations, whether oral or written.
